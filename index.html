Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. No Data Collection

CalmShift does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your usage data remains entirely on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

All data related to your focus sessions, app usage, and preferences are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Permissions Used

We only request necessary permissions for the app to function:

  • Notification Permission: Required to send reminders and focus session alerts.

We do not use these permissions for tracking or analytics.

4. No Third-Party Access

CalmShift does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or advertisers. Your data is not shared externally.

5. User Control & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete all stored data by clearing the app’s storage from your device settings.

6. Changes to this Policy

If we make updates to this policy, we will notify users within the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us at: contact.calmshift@gmail.com
